"星期五早場" 是由一群熱愛足球的退休人士於2022年5月成立，目的為推廣黃金年代長者足球活動。 通過定期的訓練，友賽和不同的活動，從而提升體能和技術，擴闊社交圈子，促進身心健康。 由於安排之足球活動主要在星期五早上，所以稱之為 "星期五早場"。
 
"Friday Morning Show" is formed by a group of retired football enthusiast in May 2022, with the objective to promote golden age football activities. Via regular training, matches and different activities, so as to improve body fitness and techniques, broaden social circles and enhance both physical and psychological health. Since most activities are arranged in Friday morning, therefore the group is named “Friday Morning Show”.
